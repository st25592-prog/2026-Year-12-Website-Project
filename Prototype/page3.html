National Rugby League (NRL)

The National Rugby League (NRL) is the premier professional rugby league competition in Australia and New Zealand.

The NRL season runs annually and includes a regular season followed by a finals series that determines the champion.

State of Origin

State of Origin is one of the most intense and celebrated rugby league series in the world.

The series attracts millions of viewers and is widely regarded as one of the highlights of the rugby league calendar each year.

Rugby League World Cup

The Rugby League World Cup is the sports most prestigious international tournament.

Countries such as Australia, New Zealand, and England have traditionally been dominant forces in the competition.
