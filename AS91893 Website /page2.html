Maori & Tongan Cuisine

Food that connects land, sea, and people.

Maori Food (Kai)

Maori food is based on earth cooking methods like hangi, where meat and vegetables are cooked underground using hot stones.

Tongan Food

Tongan food uses cocnut cream, taro eaves, and seafood. A popular dish is 'ota 'ika, raw fish mixed with coconut cream and vegetables.

Shared Polynesian Influence

Both cultures use root vegetables like taro, kumara, and yam, and traditional earth ovens like hangi and 'umu.
